# $SPONG — Brand Playbook

> **Read before writing any Spong brief.** The Spong brand is a 2003-era
> cursed-internet lineage (Joel Veitch / rathergood.com) wrapped around
> a Solana token. Nothing about this brand should look 2026.

## The One-Line Thesis

A spongmonkey is a low-fi photo cutout of a monkey head with HUGE
googly eyes, singing sincerely about something mundane, pasted onto a
crude MSPaint body in a cursed flat-illustrated scene.

If any of those four words is missing — *low-fi, sincere, cursed,
flat-illustrated* — the piece is not Spong.

## The Three Non-Negotiables

1. **SPONG THE EYES.** The word "spong" itself is the verb for adding
   oversized white eyes with tiny black pupils. If the eyes are not
   comically too big, the piece fails. No exceptions.
2. **PHOTO HEAD + ILLUSTRATED BODY.** The head must be a real-photo
   cutout (blurry, matte edges, obviously pasted). The body must be a
   flat illustrated drawing. The mismatch is the joke.
3. **2003 AESTHETIC.** JPEG compression, yellowed background, crude
   linework, Comic Sans captions. No crisp 4K, no clean vector, no
   modern typography, no AI-smooth skin.

## Sincerity Rule

Spongmonkeys never wink. They are not in on the joke. They
sincerely love the moon, the subs, the yacht, the oval office. Any
caption written with irony, self-awareness, or meta-commentary breaks
the illusion.

Bad: *"when the bag hits and we all know it's cursed."*
Good: *"we like the moon."*

## The Quiznos Benchmark

In 2004, Quiznos paid for national broadcast time and aired these
cursed singing monkeys at the Super Bowl. 30,000+ customers called to
complain in the first week. That is the standard the brand is chasing:
*content so sincerely cursed that a corporate sponsor willingly paid to
put it in front of 100 million Americans and half of them didn't know
what they'd just seen.*

Every piece should carry that energy.

## Prompt Scaffolds (cross-reference data/formats/library.yaml)

### Solo spongmonkey, scene-based

```
A cursed 2003-Flash-animation style still: SPONG.monkey_head_cutout
with SPONG.spong_eyes and SPONG.singing_mouth, attached to
SPONG.rathergood_illustration body wearing [outfit], placed in
[scene]. Background: SPONG.jpeg_2003_compression. Caption:
SPONG.we_like_the_moon (or a sincere riff on it). Absolutely not 4K,
not clean, not photoreal.
```

### Quiznos-style ad parody

```
SPONG.quiznos_broadcast_still: a single spongmonkey centered in the
frame, singing to camera with broadcast lower-third lyrics. The
character does not know it is selling anything — the sincerity makes
it cursed. SPONG.spong_eyes + SPONG.singing_mouth required.
```

### Duet / trio

```
SPONG.duet_trio_arrangement in SPONG.canonical_scene. All spongmonkeys
share SPONG.spong_eyes + SPONG.singing_mouth. Fur colors differ: e.g.
normal + pink + rainbow. Caption: harmonized chorus line.
```

## Kill List (the things that will break a Spong piece)

- Clean high-res rendering. The vibe REQUIRES jpeg compression.
- Cute / marketable design choices. Spong is cursed, not cute.
- Small eyes. Eyes must read as spong from a thumbnail.
- Ironic captions. Every caption is sincere.
- Modern typography (Inter, Helvetica Neue, SF Pro). Anachronistic.
- AI-smooth skin on the photo head cutout. It must read 2003.
- Correcting the "wrong" proportions. Eyes too big is the point.
- A spongmonkey without the singing mouth. They ARE singing.

## Cross-brand note

Do NOT conflate with $SPONGE (the SpongeBob memecoin). Different
