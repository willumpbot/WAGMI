## 1. What 160k records taught us (ranked)
1. **The problem is upstream signal generation, not gates.** No confidence threshold makes the live stream positive: best slice −9bps/4h gross vs ~10bps fees. Gate-tuning is rearranging deck chairs.
2. **Confidence does not rank outcomes.** Era-split AUC 0.43–0.51 (pooled 0.537 = wk1 artifact). 60–79 anti-signal CONFIRMED at n=926: conf 70–79 = −150bps/24h. Live floors 66/71 select TOWARD the worst slice (kept −141.8 vs blocked −67.6 bps/24h). EV falls monotonically with floor above ~50; a 65 CEILING beats every floor.
3. **Week-1 crash beta contaminates every "gates burn alpha" claim.** W1 SELL rejections: 92% WR / +385bps. Every gate that "denied edge" (low_consensus +585→−138, low_confidence +409→−65, daily_limit +285→~0) dies ex-W1. Beta, not gates.
4. **Denominators were inflated all along.** 79k rejections → 5,528 unique episodes (41% dupes); 56k signals → 6,967 episodes (8x 30s re-emission). DATA_CENSUS record counts are fake denominators.
5. **Only REGIME has skill.** Trend calls 58.5% @4h (n=641) vs mech classifier 47.5%; survives all 3 eras (.648/.538/.592). Everything else is wk1 artifact or noise.
6. **volume_chop is a broken, expensive instrument.** Fires on logged value 0.0 vs threshold 0.5, 59% of all rejections, below-base precision, uniquely blocked +36.6bps/24h signals (n=1,620). Also redundant: 63% of conf-floor rejects already chop-rejected (Jaccard 0.43).
7. **TRADE skip filter is real post-wk1** (skips would avg −0.27% mid / −0.39% late, n=2,661 CF-matched); go entries have no skill ex-wk1 (mid −33bps @4h); go-confidence INVERTED (corr −0.17).
8. **CRITIC veto value is a wk1 artifact** — mid-era ordering inverted (vetoed −79 vs approved −196bps @24h). **EXIT**: full_closes timely (+3.3bps left, n=2,749); holds bad 2/3 eras (−25bps @4h).
9. **Quant Brain suspicion CONFIRMED twice.** ev_per_dollar prompt stat AUC 0.444–0.470 (anti-predictive); QUANT's "+75bps short edge" = pure wk1 (+311 → −22 → −1); noise-tag has zero separating power. 2,721 Opus calls wasted.
10. **Two sniper gates provably save money**: quality_floor_proven_solo −70bps raw (BUY −227, n=428); aggressive_standard_skip LATE −179 (n=439); dangerous_regime_highvol earned its W1 keep (excess −283). Keep all sniper gates.
11. **Measurement is broken where it matters**: 7/9 agent roles log constant 0.5 confidence; go→ledger match 15.7%; side inference era-biased (57% wk1 → 9% late); rejections never logged entry/SL/TP. Instrument fixes precede any further agent/gate conclusions.
12. Dead wiring: trend_alignment gate 0 firings in 52,954 evals; rr/fee/ev floors 1–2 firings — unwired. llm_skip disjoint, no positive selection (exec n=102 −20bps vs skip +18).
